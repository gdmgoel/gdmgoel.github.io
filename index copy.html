Hello! I am Dhruv, and I am a graduate student in mathematics from Mumbai. I'm broadly interested in algebraic and differential geometry, although I have yet to find a subfield of math that I dislike!

I did my undergraduate studies at Harvard, where I was in Lowell House. I wrote my senior thesis on applications of the Atiyah-Singer Index Theorem under the supervision of Joe Harris and Dan Freed.

For the 2024-25 academic year, I will be at Trinity College, Cambridge, for Part III of the Mathematical Tripos.

Outside of math, I like to sing, write music, read (mostly sci-fi, high fantasy, and horror), and watch Black Mirror.
