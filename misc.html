Miscellaneous

Math Community

I was a member of Harvard Undergraduate Gender Inclusivity in Math from 2021 to 2023. I served as the co-president for AY 2022-23, and as the mentorships co-chair for the AY 2021-22.

Singing

  • I was a member of the Harvard University Choir from 2022 to 2024, and a Ferris Choral Fellow for the AY 2023-24.
  • I was a member of the Harvard Glee Club from 2019 to 2024, and in Glee Club Lite for the AY 2021-22.
  • In February 2022, I played the baritone role of Bard in the premier of Harry Sage’s If a Picture Never Lies, an operatic adaptation of Virgil’s Bucolics, Eclogues 1, 2, and 9. The Harvard Crimson published an article about it.

Compositions

As a member of the Harvard Choruses New Music Initiative from 2022 to 2024, I wrote:
  1. Daffodils, a setting of Wordsworth’s poem I Wandered Lonely as a Cloud for the Harvard Glee Club. This piece premiered in the American Cathedral in Paris during our Europe tour in January 2024. Here is a recording of us performing the piece Sanders Theatre, conducted by me.
  2. I Sing Because I Can for Cambridge Common Voices.
In addition:
  1. As part of my final project for Music 51B: Theory Ib at Harvard in Spring 2021, I wrote a piece for string quartet titled You Are What You Sing, which was premiered by the Grammy Award-winning Parker Quartet.
